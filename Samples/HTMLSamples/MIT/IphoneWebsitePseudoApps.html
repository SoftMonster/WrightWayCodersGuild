Add a Webpage Shortcut to Your iPhone Home Screen

Follow these simple steps to quickly add any webpage to your iPhone Home Screen:

  • 🔹 Open Safari on your iPhone
  • 🔹 Go to the webpage you want to save
  • 🔹 Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow)
  • 🔹 Choose "Add to Home Screen"
  • 🔹 Name the shortcut whatever you like
  • 🔹 Tap "Add" and it will appear on your Home Screen like an app!

Designed for clarity and quick access—now your favorite pages are always just a tap away!
