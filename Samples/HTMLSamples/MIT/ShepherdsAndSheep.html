Be a sheep, be a shepherd

We are all shepherds and sheep. Influencers and followers.

🧠🐑 Recognise drift as default human condition, not exception
“We all, like sheep, have gone astray…” (Isaiah 53:6)
“You were like sheep going astray…” (1 Peter 2:25)

🔁🐑 Return mentally to alignment before making decisions
⚠️🐑 Treat independence-from-guidance as high-risk state
🧍🐑 Accept correction without resistance when exposed
