🌿 Minimalist Spending 🌿

Minimalist spending is a lifestyle approach focusing on intentional purchases and prioritizing experiences over material possessions. It involves:

  • 🛍️ Evaluating needs vs. wants
  • 💸 Setting a budget and sticking to it
  • 🧹 Decluttering and simplifying possessions
  • 🛒 Making mindful purchases
  • 🌱 Embracing quality over quantity

By adopting minimalist spending habits, individuals aim to reduce financial stress, increase savings, and find contentment in living with less.

✅ Survival Tips

🥫 Food

  • Buy cheap staples (oats, rice, lentils, etc.)
  • Cook from scratch, avoid snacks or brands
  • Drink tap water only

🏠 Home

  • Heat one room only
  • Use washbowls or short showers
  • Air-dry clothes, hand-wash if needed

📶 Connectivity

  • Use SIM plans like Lebara or Giffgaff
  • Use free WiFi in libraries or cafés

🚌 Transport

  • Walk or cycle only
  • Use bus discounts where available
